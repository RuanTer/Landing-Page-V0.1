## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

**Images not showing?**
- Check filenames match exactly (case-sensitive)
- Verify images are in `/images/services/` folder
- Make sure file extension is `.jpg` not `.jpeg`
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl + F5)

**Images look pixelated?**
- Download higher resolution (160x160 minimum)
- Use JPG quality 85% or higher
- Avoid upscaling smaller images

**Images too large?**
- Compress with TinyPNG
- Reduce quality to 80%
- Convert PNG to JPG
